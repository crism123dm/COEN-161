Open Graph



            The use of open graph protocol is to display your webpage into a graph object. If someone needs to 
            access your webpage in the background, these tags will make the link to the document look more
            appealing.
            
            The open graph tags are inserted into the head of the document to be used as metadata. 

            The prefix for twitter is "twitter" instead of "og".
